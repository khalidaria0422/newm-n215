SUPPORTING MEANINGFUL CAUSES

A donation is made for every 10 toys purchased.

Riley Cheer Guild

Donations are made to the Riley Children's Hospital and IU Health. Donated toys are used for holidays, birthdays, and other special occasions.

Ronald McDonald House

Donatoins are made to the Riley Children's Hospital and other pediatric hospitals. The Ronald McDonald House has a holiday wishlist, and together, we can make those wishes come true.

Toys For Tots

Donations are distributed as Christmas gifts to the families in need in the community.

WRTV Toy Drive

Serving the Hoosier families. Toy donations will benefit Grace Care Center, Urban Act Academy, Project Angel, Amp Harris Foundation, Churches in Mission, Servants Heart of Indy, Lawrence Firefighters Family Assistance Program, and The Chin Community of Indiana.
