Our promise

Why Adventure With Us?

Trail & Timber was founded by a pair of wilderness guides who believed that the outdoors should be accessible, safe, and deeply meaningful — not just a checkbox on a bucket list.

Every trip is designed with intention. We match terrain to skill level, keep groups intimate, and ensure our guides know the land as well as they know their guests.

  • IFMGA-certified mountain guides with 10+ years field experience
  • Small groups of 8 or fewer for a personal, immersive experience
  • Industry-leading safety protocols and real-time weather monitoring
  • Premium gear: Black Diamond, MSR, and Patagonia equipment
  • Leave No Trace certified — we protect the places we explore
  • 24/7 emergency support on every guided trip
